## Essential Listings

### 1. Google Business Profile (Highest Priority)

**Why**: 90% of local searches happen on Google. This is your #1 priority.

**Setup Steps**:
1. Go to [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com)
2. Sign in with your business Google account
3. Click "Add business" or "Claim this business"
4. Enter your business name: "NorthStack Solutions"
5. Choose business category: "Information Technology Company" or "Computer Support and Services"
6. Add your business location:
   - If you serve customers at your location: Add your full address
   - If you serve customers at their location: Choose "I serve customers at their location"
   - Service area: Calgary, Alberta, Canada (expand as needed)
7. Add contact information:
   - Phone: Your business phone number
   - Website: https://northstack.ca
8. Complete verification (Google will send a postcard or offer phone/email verification)

**Optimization Checklist**:
- ‚úÖ Complete all profile sections (100% completion)
- ‚úÖ Add business description (750 characters max)
- ‚úÖ Upload logo and cover photo
- ‚úÖ Add 10+ high-quality photos (office, team, work examples)
- ‚úÖ Set business hours (including holidays)
- ‚úÖ Add services with descriptions
- ‚úÖ Create posts weekly (updates, offers, news)
- ‚úÖ Enable messaging for customer inquiries
- ‚úÖ Add products (if applicable)
- ‚úÖ Respond to all reviews within 24-48 hours
- ‚úÖ Use Google Posts for announcements and offers

**Key Information to Include**:
```

Phone: +1-403-XXX-XXXX
```

### 2. Bing Places for Business

**Why**: 10-15% of searches happen on Bing. Easy to set up, often overlooked by competitors.

**Setup**:
1. Visit [Bing Places for Business](https://www.bingplaces.com)
2. Sign in with Microsoft account
3. Add or claim your business
4. Import from Google Business Profile (saves time!)
5. Verify your listing

**Key Features**:
- Automatically syncs with Apple Maps
- Less competition than Google
- Growing market share in enterprise searches

---

### 3. Apple Maps (Apple Business Connect)

**Why**: Essential for iPhone and Mac users (30%+ of Canadian market)

**Setup**:
1. Visit [Apple Business Connect](https://register.apple.com/placesonmaps)
2. Sign in with Apple ID
3. Search for your business or add new location
4. Complete business information
5. Verify via phone or email

**Optimization**:
- Add logo and photos
- Complete all business details
- Enable Apple Pay (if applicable)
- Monitor and respond to reviews

---

### 4. Facebook Business Page

**Why**: Social proof, customer engagement, and additional search visibility

**Setup**:
1. Go to [Facebook Pages](https://www.facebook.com/pages/create)
2. Choose "Business or Brand"
3. Fill in business details
4. Add profile and cover photos
5. Complete "About" section with keywords

**Optimization Tips**:
- Post regularly (2-3x per week minimum)
- Share blog articles and case studies
- Engage with comments and messages promptly
- Use Facebook Insights to track engagement
- Run occasional promotions or ads

## Canadian-Specific Directories

### 1. Yellow Pages Canada (YP.ca)

**Why**: Trusted Canadian directory, strong domain authority

**Setup**:
1. Visit [YP.ca](https://www.yp.ca/business-owners)
2. Search for your business
3. Claim or add your listing
4. Complete all details
5. Choose relevant categories

**Paid Options**: Consider enhanced listing for better placement

---

### 2. Canada411

**Why**: Major Canadian directory, high search volume

**Setup**:
1. Visit [Canada411 Business](https://www.canada411.ca/bus/)
2. Search and claim your listing
3. Update business information
4. Add website and contact details

---

### 3. Yelp Canada

**Why**: Customer reviews, especially important for service businesses

**Setup**:
1. Visit [Yelp for Business](https://biz.yelp.ca)
2. Claim your business
3. Complete profile details
4. Add photos and business hours
5. Respond to reviews

**Important**:
- Never ask for positive reviews (against Yelp policy)
- Always respond professionally to negative reviews
- Add high-quality photos

---

**Why**: Free Canadian business directory with good SEO value

**Action**: Add free business listing

```

```

### Optimization Tips

1. **Complete Every Field**: 100% complete profiles rank higher
2. **Use Keywords Naturally**: Include "DevOps Calgary", "IT automation", "cloud services", etc.
3. **Add Photos**: Businesses with photos get 42% more direction requests and 35% more clicks
4. **Collect Reviews**: Ask satisfied clients for reviews (Google, Facebook, LinkedIn)
5. **Update Regularly**: Keep hours current, add posts, update photos
6. **Monitor & Respond**: Reply to reviews and messages within 24 hours
7. **Track Performance**: Use analytics from each platform

### Review Management

**How to Ask for Reviews**:
```
Hi [Client Name],

We're glad you're happy with [project/service]! If you have a moment,
would you mind leaving a review on Google? It helps other Canadian
businesses find us.

Here's the link: [Google Review Link]

Thanks!
- NorthStack Solutions Team
```

**Responding to Reviews**:
- ‚úÖ Thank positive reviews: "Thanks for the kind words, [Name]!"
- ‚úÖ Address negative reviews professionally: "We're sorry to hear about your experience. Please contact us at info@northstack.ca so we can make this right."
- ‚úÖ Respond within 24-48 hours
- ‚úÖ Keep responses brief and professional
